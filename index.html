    Our Equipment

    We provide top-notch hockey equipment, including sticks, skates, and protective gear, ensuring our members have access to high-quality gear for a safe and effective training experience.

    Hockey Training

    Our training sessions are expertly crafted, focusing on skill development, strategic gameplay, and fitness. Sessions are designed to cater to all skill levels, ensuring every member can enhance their game.

    Private Lessons

    Scheduling private lessons is easy! Members can contact our coaching staff directly or use our online booking system to choose a convenient time for their one-on-one sessions.

    Ski Touring

    Ski touring is a fantastic cross-training option, enhancing overall fitness and endurance. It provides a refreshing break from the ice while maintaining a focus on physical conditioning.

    Booking

    Members can book sessions up to two weeks in advance, ensuring they have ample time to plan their training schedule and secure their preferred time slots.

    Pricings

    The membership fee covers access to all regular training sessions, use of club equipment, and exclusive discounts on private lessons. It's an all-inclusive package for a comprehensive training experience.
